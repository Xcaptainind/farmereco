Farmers' Empowerment Platform

Protecting Indian farmers from exploitation and suicide due to low MSP, our open-source platform connects farmers directly with buyers, ensuring fair prices and wider reach. (Source: [1] "Farmers' Suicides in India" by The Hindu)

Login via phone number and Aadhar verification, farmers can sell crops at optimal prices, preventing spoilage and middleman exploitation. (Reference: [2] "Minimum Support Price" by Government of India)

With a dark theme, animations, and styles, our platform prioritizes user experience and security.
